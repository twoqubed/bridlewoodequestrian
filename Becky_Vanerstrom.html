Becky Vanerstrom

Barn Manager/Trainer 




Becky Vanerstrom, a lifelong horsewoman, began her riding career on the Illinois hunter/jumper circuit. After training under such esteemed professionals as Rush and Caroline Weeden, Alex R. Jayne, and Katie Monahan-Prudent and competing in top notch horse shows such as the Winter Equestrian Festival, the National Horse Show and Capital Challenge, she began managing her own private farm in 1999.

 


Becky joined the Bridlewood team in October 2011 after moving to Texas from Illinois.  She brings over 30 years of experience riding,showing and teaching to Bridlewood.
