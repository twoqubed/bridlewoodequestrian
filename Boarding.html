Facilities & Boarding

The fully renovated facilities at Bridlewood Stables & Equestrian Center (BSEC) make this the perfect home for your treasured equine. Situated on 12 beautiful acres in Flower Mound, Texas, we offer big-city convenience wrapped in a tranquil, pastoral setting. When you first pass through our gates, you will note the beautiful paddock areas. Your horse will have the luxury of spending time outside in an individual paddock suitable for even the fanciest show horse/pony. If training and lessons are in your plans, note the abundance of working arenas. Bridlewood offers a lighted, covered arena for riding during evening hours and inclement weather. We have two beautiful new hunter courses with updated footing for your horse's safety and comfort. Inside the barn, you and your horse will be pampered with oversized (12’ x 12’) stalls, wash racks with hot and cold running water, an air-conditioned boarder lounge, snack area, tack room, laundry facilities, and much, much more. Our trained barn staff, on-site and around-the-clock, enable us to provide a level of horse care worthy of the most discriminating owner. With first-class amenities like clean, oversized stalls and an exceptional nutrition program, your horse will thrive as a member of the BSEC family.
