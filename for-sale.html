Harlequins Romance

"Harley" is a 15.2 15 year old appendix. They don't make them more kind or honest than this guy! Will win the local circuit all day long and holds his own in the limit/modifieds at A shows. Quiet enough for walk trot, but happy to teach his kid the ropes in the modifieds and well schooled enough to learn lateral work on. If there were any way to keep this guy in the barn, I would! No prep, easy kind of guy.

Silver Star's Jumpin Jack

2002 large Welsh pony. "Jack" is a kind, in your pocket pony suitable for canter crossrails through 2'6". He's the same pony away as at home, great to trail ride and play around on, and cute enough to show. Everyone loves him, he just needs his on kid!

Charlie Bucket

11 year old 15.1 paint gelding. Currently coursing 2'3". Very honest to his fences. He doesn't love lesson horse life and would prefer to have one person. Suitable project for a teenager working with a trainer.
