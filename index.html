Welcome to Bridlewood Stables and Equestrian Center

Located in the Bridlewood development of Flower Mound, Texas, Bridlewood Stables and Equestrian Center (BSEC) is a family owned and operation boarding and teaching facility. A long-standing member of the Flower Mound community, the center was purchased by the McKenzie family and fully renovated to an ADA compatible facility in 2007.

Nestled with the Bridlewood Farms neighborhood, you will find this pristine gem with over 45 stalls, 16 turnouts, a covered riding arena, trainers with 20+ years of experience teaching all age gourps and levels, a highly successful therapeutic riding program, and so much more.
