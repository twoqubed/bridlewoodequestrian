Harlequins Romance

Harley is as simple and straightforward as they come! Simple enough for a beginner, but well schooled enough to take a young rider through modified hunters. He makes absolutely everyone look good- we just adore this guy! He has taken his current rider from short stirrup through the modifieds and is looking for his next kid! Harley is truly a barn favorite with a lovely attitude and work ethic- he doesn't know how to say "no". He has multiple champions at the local circuit level and will hold his own at A/AA shows- he was second in the Bit o' Straw Classic at the 2016 GO Show. He is perfect for a kid looking to step up from ponies and get involved in showing. No prep at home or away, easy keeper and on the ground.

Charlie Bucket

Charlie has been a part of our lesson program for the last several years and is looking for a kid of his own. He would excel with a spunky kid looking for something to bring along with a trainer. Charlie is very game and fun to ride and loves to jump around- very honest to his fences! He is very low maintenance and easy to have in the barn. Priced in the low-mid four figures.
