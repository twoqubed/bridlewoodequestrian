Cruise Control "Cruz"

2007 16.2 bay Thoroughbred gelding "Cruz" has been a well-loved part of our lesson program, but we feel he would excel with his own rider. Still fairly green, he is completing coursework and started on changes and will be ready to show in the baby greens for 2018. He has been hauled to school off property and is the same level-headed guy away from home as he is at home. He would be a super project for a young teen looking for a project! Priced in the four figures. Video coming soon!

He has a fabulous willing attitude with auto changes and an easy ride to his fences. This guy is a very athletic and capable jumper ready for his next kid! Priced in the low five figures. Northrop

2010 16.0 bay APHA gelding. "JJ" is an absolute barn favorite with his puppy-dog personality- he will happily follow his person around the ring! This guy is absolutely unflappable and loves attention- he will excel as a kids horse. Goes in a happy mouth bit and definitely is more of a kick ride. JJ is very honest to his fences and jumps with textbook style every time. He has plenty of local circuit miles and is ready to head to the A shows in the greens. Really only needs show miles- he would be wonderful for a kid to finish out! Video is from December 2010, changes have developed since then. Updated video coming soon! Priced in the low five figures.

Ronaldo

2005 13.1 7/8 grey Welsh pony gelding. They don't make them kinder than this pony! He will happily pack around a walk/trot beginner, and then go do the division with another kid. He has easy changes, is super honest to his fences, and does not know how to be rude or unkind. If we had a kid for him, he wouldn't be leaving! Ronaldo has had limited showing in the last year due to no fault of his own, but is ready to go with a new kid! Available for sale or lease, sale price in the mid five figures.
