Jenna Vannerstrom

Trainer




Jenna began her riding career in Illinois, but quickly joined the ranks of top junior riders, competing at venues such as the Winter Equestrian Festival, the Kentucky Horse Park, the Pennsylvania National Horse Show, and many more. As a junior, Jenna competed with great success in hunter, jumper and equitation divisions, from the medium pony division all the way to the Gran Prix show jumping level. She has competed in prestigious invitational events such as the USET Talent Search East finals, and even placed in the top 25 at the USEF Medal Final in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania.

 

I
n addition to her success in competition, Jenna brings a wealth of professional experience to Bridlewood. She has worked as a groom and rider for elite trainers on the East Coast and in France, and has spent the last 5 years working under Olympic show jumper and coach extraordinaire Katie Monahan Prudent. This tenure at Plain Bay Farm has given Jenna an extensive knowledge base in horse management, which she is excited to bring to Bridlewood.

 

Jenna also has proven abilities as a trainer. She has coached junior riders to top ribbons in the high junior jumpers in the Prix de States at the Pennsylvania National Horse Show, the National Horse Show in Syracuse, New York, the Washington International Horse Show, and the North American Young Riders Championships. In addition, she has brought along many young horses in both the hunter and jumper rings.
